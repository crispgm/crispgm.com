About me

I am David Zhang, graduated from Southeast University and later Queensland University of Technology. Currently, I work as an engineer for Baidu Inc. I mostly use and are familiar with PHP, awk, shell, nginx and C/C++.

This is my personal blog, built with open source power like Github Pages, Markdown and PHP. The theme of the pages is inspired by ArchLinux.org. It is mostly about computer stuffs, especially on programming tips and sharing of fantastic tools.

I enjoy simplicity and minimalism. Exploring fantastic softwares and apps is my favorite hobby. And I make use of them, including Chrome, vim, GitHub, SublimeText, Foobar 2000, Wunderlist, Evernote, Pocket and Instagram. In addition, I enjoy building web pages, customized configurations, software extensions and utility scripts. In non-programming spare time, I like reading books with Kindle and girls wearing pantyhose.
